My DawaiWala Grievance Redressal
Policy
1. PURPOSE
The purpose of this Grievance Redressal Policy is to provide a
transparent and eﬃcient mechanism for addressing and resolving
customer grievances related to the delivery of medicines within 20
minutes of the order time.
2. SCOPE
This policy applies to all customers of My DawaiWala who have
placed orders for medicines through our online platform and have not
received their deliveries within the promised timeframe of 20 minutes
from the order time.
3. GRIEVANCE REDRESSAL PROCESS
3.1 Submission of Grievance
Customers who have not received their medicine deliveries within 20
minutes of the order time may submit a grievance through one of the
following channels:
●Call our customer support hotline at [Customer
Support Number].
●Send an email to infodawaiwala@gmail.com.
●Use the "Grievance Redressal" feature on our website
to submit a written complaint.
3.2 Investigation and Resolution
●Upon receiving a grievance, our customer support team will
initiate an investigation into the matter.
●We will prioritize resolving grievances related to delayed
deliveries within the shortest possible time frame.
●Our team will communicate with relevant stakeholders,
including delivery personnel and logistics partners, to
determine the cause of the delay.
●Once the cause of the delay is identiﬁed, we will take
appropriate corrective actions to resolve the issue and
ensure timely delivery of the medicines.
●We will keep the customer informed about the status of
their grievance and the steps being taken to address it.
3.3 Escalation
●If a customer is dissatisﬁed with the resolution provided by
our customer support team, they may escalate the
grievance to the Grievance Redressal Oﬃcer.
●The Grievance Redressal Oﬃcer will conduct a thorough
review of the grievance and the actions taken to resolve it.
●The Grievance Redressal Oﬃcer will communicate the ﬁnal
decision to the customer and take any additional steps
necessary to address the grievance.
4. RECORD KEEPING
●We will maintain records of all grievances received and the
actions taken to resolve them.
●These records will be kept conﬁdential and used for
internal review and improvement of our services.
5. REVIEW AND IMPROVEMENT
●We will periodically review our grievance redressal process
to identify areas for improvement.
●Customer feedback and suggestions will be taken into
consideration to enhance the effectiveness of our
grievance redressal mechanism.
6. CONTACT US
If you have any questions or concerns about our Grievance Redressal
Policy, please contact us at [Contact Information]
